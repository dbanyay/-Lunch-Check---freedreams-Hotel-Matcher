Freedreams Hotels + Lunch-Check

This mini-project aims find Swiss hotels where you can stay for free (pay only for meals) with Freedreams hotel vouchers and use Lunch-Check🇨🇭 credits to cover those meals—turning your getaway into a nearly free trip!

📋 How to Book (Follow These Steps):

  1. Double-check the match: Mistakes can happen during the automatic process - verify the hotel really has the restaurant listed.
  2. Contact the hotel directly: Ask if they have rooms available for your dates AND confirm you can pay for meals with Lunch-Check.
  3. Buy your Freedreams voucher: Visit freedreams.ch/de/shop
  4. Book with the hotel on Freedream's site: Use your Freedreams voucher to secure your stay!
